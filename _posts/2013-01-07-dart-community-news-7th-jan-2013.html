Happy new year to all you all, and welcome to the first community news of 2013.

Dart M2 is starting to get bedded in, and the new streams API has just been merged from the experimental branch into bleeding_edge branch.

The Dart community hasn't been on holiday either - I think people have been taking advantage of the extra time to create more excellent Dart code.
