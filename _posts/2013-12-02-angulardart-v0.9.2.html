--- title: 'AngularDart v0.9.2' layout: post tags: { } category: angulardart published: '2013-12-02T16:33:00-08:00' --- AngularDart release v0.9.2 limited-omnipotence is now available!

This release includes parser improvements like support for ternary/conditional and integer division operators, feature and performance improvements in scope like early exiting of digest loop, scope.$watchSet API, scope.$disabled, lazy scope digest and support for skipping auto-digest. This release also includes a number of fixes and improvements to static parser generator as well as experimental support for faster ng-shallow-repeat.

As always, for full details of this release see the changelog.

Thanks to everyone who contributed PRs for this release: Chris Bracken, Graham MacDonald and Justin Fagnani.
