Welcome to Dartery! I'm Justin and this is where I'm going to chronicle my adventures in Dart land.


I doubt anyone visiting this site won't know already, but Dart is a new programming language project started by my employer - Google - with the goal of making web development much better for large projects and large teams.

Dart is in it's very early stages and there's still a lot of work to be done, a lot of code to be written, and a lot of patterns, best practices and idioms to figure out. That makes things fairly exciting, and increases the need for Darters to share their experiences to each other and the Dart team, and that's exactly my goal with this blog.
